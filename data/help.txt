Commands: 

    -help (You are currently looking at the help page)

    -list (Shows outstanding tasks)
    -find (Displays all tasks that contain a given search term)

    -mark (Declare an existing task as complete)
    -unmark (Declare an existing task as incomplete)
    -delete (Delete an existing task)

    -todo (Create a task todo)
    -deadline (Create a task to complete by a deadline)
    -event (Create a task to attend to on a specific timing)

    -bye (Close Duke application)
